Summary

Tekken 7 is the newest in the series of a classic 3D area style fighter. Tekken is known for its wacky cast of characters and its crazy accurate hitboxes allowing for some amazing close combat. Ducking, dodging, and weaving out of combos is key to defensive play and counter attacking in Tekken, which makes this game a bit more offputting to the casual player. Nonetheless, Tekken 7 is an amazing fighter that lives up to all expectations, and is definately a show!
